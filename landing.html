About me

Hello, I am Ivan a second year student at the University of Colorado at Boulder majoring in Computer Science with a Minor in Creative Technology and Design. I am passionate about technology and want to use technology to make the world a better place for everyone. My technical interests lie in web development, game development, and hardware development I am eager to learn more about these fields and want to focus on these things for the rest of my degree. Some technical skills i know include Photoshop, Figma, C++, Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, which have been developed through both coursework and personal projects. Out of all my computer science classes taken at Boulder, my favorite class so far has been algorithms, because solving lots of algorithm problems remind me of solving puzzles, which are always fun. I am interested in design because I believe that it is a way of self-expression and I enjoy making things look aesthetically pleasing, and is the reason why I chose to minor in creative technology and design. I am also interested in cloud computing, specifically what involves the storing and managing of information across multiple systems and being able to access this information anywhere as long as you have a Wifi connection. I am interested in these fields as I think they are key components in todays world for change, and make people lives easier. Outside of school, I enjoy playing tennis, going to the gym, creating writing, and fixing old computers. Thank you for your time and with all this being said I am excited about the future and look forward to contributing to the tech industry.
